### H1. choch_exit MFE gate

**Problem:** `core/execution.py` lines 495–501 fires CHoCH-exit on any single-bar structure flip, regardless of whether the trade has moved in favor. ENA short on 2026-04-29 16:10 was scratched at -$30 by a single bullish wick that immediately reversed; price closed below entry on the very next bar.

**Hypothesis:** Honor `choch_exit` only when `max_favorable_atr >= 0.5 * sl_atr` (i.e. trade has put in real work first). Below that threshold, treat the structure flip as noise — let SL handle it.

**Counterfactual replay (2026-04-29, n=7):**
- Baseline live PnL on choch trades: -$150
- Variant A (MFE >= 0.5R): +$50 (+$200 swing)
- BUT swing dominated by one XRP outlier (+$521). Strip it → variant is net negative. **Sample too small to trust.**

**Action items:**
1. Port `choch_exit` logic into `research/commod_backtest.py` (currently only modeled in live execution).
2. Run forward-walk validation on all 9 active configs with the MFE-gate variant.
3. Pass criteria: gate.json — n>=15 per window, PF>=1.5, all 5 windows pass, beat baseline by >0.3 PF.
4. Also test variant B (confirmation bar — require next-bar continuation) and variant C (min-displacement >= 0.3 ATR) for completeness.
5. Counterfactual script at `/tmp/choch_variants_backtest.py` (move to `research/` if work resumes).

**Status:** queued, no deployment until forward-walk passes.

### H2. Entry-timing filter — don't fade into stretched oversold/overbought

**Problem:** ETH 2026-04-29 17:00 UTC. The bot shorted at $2270.20 — the local low of a 3% selloff — with 5m RSI at 38 (just bouncing off 29). 1h trend was down, but 5m structure was already turning up. Position was scratched in 1 bar (choch_exit -$57) and again in 0 bars after manual re-open (ensemble_exit -$22).

**Hypothesis:** The bot is selling micro-bottoms / buying micro-tops because the entry triggers fire at the moment ensemble consensus *just* reaches K=4 — which is also the moment most likely to be a local extreme. Adding an entry-side anti-extreme filter should cut these scratches without losing legitimate entries.

**Filter candidates (test all three):**
1. **5m RSI gate** — block short entries when 5m RSI < 35; block long entries when 5m RSI > 65 at entry bar.
2. **Distance-from-mean gate** — require entry price within X×ATR of 1h EMA-50. Filters overextended entries that are mean-reverting.
3. **Down-leg-already-moved gate** — block short entries when last N bars have already moved more than Y% against you (cumulative selloff into your direction).

**Audit data (2026-04-29, n=69 active universe trades):**
- 9 trades (13%) closed in ≤3 bars on choch/regime/structural exits
- Net -$51 across all 9
- Concentrated on TIA (2), PENDLE (2), XRP (2 — both winners), ETH (1), BTC (1), ENA (1)
- HYPE, ZEC: 0 premature exits — clean PASS 5/5 forward-walk
- Pattern most painful: short-into-oversold and long-into-overbought; both are mean-reversion-bait fade entries

**Action items:**
1. Compute RSI / distance-from-EMA50 / cumulative-N-bar-move at every historical entry bar (back-derive from candles).
2. Bucket existing trades: which ones entered with RSI<35 (short) or >65 (long)? What was their PF vs the rest?
3. If the filtered subset has clearly worse PF, add filter to whale_swing strategy.
4. Forward-walk on all 9 active configs with the filter applied.
5. Pass criteria: improves PF by ≥0.3 OR reduces premature-exit count by ≥30% without dropping n below gate threshold.

**Status:** queued, post-freeze. Don't act now — sample size of 9 scratches across 69 trades is too small to justify mid-freeze changes.
